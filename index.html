Bones with Built-In LESS Compilation

Bones-LESS is a fork of Eddie Machado's excellent Bones starter theme for WordPress. What makes Bones-LESS different is that we have added Robert O'Rourke's wp-less compilation utility directly into the theme, meaning that you can get up and running with Bones without needing any special handling for the LESS stylesheets: They'll compile themselves when you visit the site! Just download the archive, unpack to your /themes directory, and go crazy.

About

The Bones-LESS and Bones-SCSS forks of Bones are maintained by the Boston WordPress community. To get involved, visit us on Github, or come hang out in person at one of our Meetups!
